Memory Machines and the Human Witness

A Multimodal Documentary

About This Documentary

This is a multimodal documentary film that incorporates archival footage, ai generated video and experimentation to explore the relationship between photographs and memory. I used generative AI video in order to evoke an emotional response from the audience. This emotional response was an effort to prove that images, especially the moving image, has the unique power to catalyze a human response to other human beings in an image, synthetic or not. The audience for this film are the technologically curious, documentarians, archivists, and anyone who holds on to the idea that photographs of lost loved ones are sacred artifcacts.
